NYU | ITPodcast

At ITP, we come from different countries all over the world, speak different languages, and consider different ideas. We are architects, dancers, mathematicians, and artists of every kind—and for some reason, we all ended up here. From the 4th floor at Tisch, we’re bringing you individual stories about the makers and thinkers of ITP as we all learn, create, and, of course, occasionally fail. Join us this semester as we go beyond the ten-second class intro.
